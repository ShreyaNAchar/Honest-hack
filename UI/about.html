About the app

Let us put our hands together to fight against corruption

Corruption is a huge bane to our Indian development. So as we are the citizens of our Nation we must develop some appropriate solutions to this so that it will curb this menace, hence our app AlertMaster. AlertMaster enables every citizen to bring any form of corruption to the notice of appropriate higher authorities and to the general public. Users can also report on things that needs government intervention?

Report it

Simply fill out a report form by entering details about the incident. You may choose to stay anonymous.

Get Feedback

We would investigate & submit your report to the appropriate authorities, giving you feedback each step of the way.

Share Report

You may share your report on social media to help spread the word and stop similar cases from occuring.
